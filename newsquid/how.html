How does it work?

With Newsquid you will be able to select which pages or directories to make pay-only and which to leave free and how much you want to charge for the individual pages. Newsquid does the rest. We collect payments, handle fraud protection and security compliance, even manage subscriber support all done via the Newsquid API.

Please contact us to learn about Newsquid micro payment service and we will help you setup the service.
